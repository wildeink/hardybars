What are Hardy Bars?

Hardy Bars are the perfect snack or meal-skipper for anyone from any age, especially athletes. Packed with goodness like Rolled Oats, Brown Rice, Walnuts, Almonds, Raisins and Peanut Butt, along with boosters like Coconut Oil and Honey, they will give you a natural boost of energy, and most of all, make you feel Hardy!

Development

Over 6 months of development went into Hardy Bars before they were distributed: this was to ensure not only a top quality bar every time, but having consistent suppliers from every angle. Closing 2019 out with distribution now in 5 cities countrywide with almost 50 locations, 2020 is expected to be huge!

Why People Love Hardy Bars

Today's consumers are stepping away from chocolate bars and look for healthier snack or meal replacement options. They are also wary of big brand bars that have hidden sugars or other ingredients that might not be fresh and healthy. Hardy Bars offer the perfect alternative to that by being All Natural with No Added Sugar.

Retailers

Retailers often have small margins and rely on a large stock of many goods to offer consumers in order to profit. With Hardy Bars, retailers are offered a chance to make much larger margins than usual and have great potential to move the bars when placed in the right position within each store.
